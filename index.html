The Foundation

The Priestly Core

Before we are sent, we are formed.

This is the center of our existence. It is not optional; it is the heartbeat of the Tribe. Whether you are a leader, an instrumentalist, a designer, or a volunteer, you belong here first.

We prioritize Prayer, Scripture, Character, Community, and Accountability. We believe that public expression must be fueled by private consecration.

Expressions of One Calling

We are not separate departments. We are one body with distinct expressions of service.

II. Sound & Service

Instrumentalists serving believers' meetings. Hosting God’s presence with humility and discipline.

"First a priest in formation, then a musician in service."

III. Formation & Youth

A safe, prayerful community for teenagers and young people. No isolation, no abandonment.

"You belong here before you achieve anything."

IV. City Engagement

Extending love to the city through outreaches and peaceable engagement.

"Seek the peace of the city... for in its peace shall ye have peace."

V. Visual Stewardship

Producing design and merchandise that reflect excellence and dignity.

"Design is our tool, not our testimony."
